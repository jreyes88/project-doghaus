The full Dog Haüs experience

We'll take care of you and your pooch!

Established in 2016, the Dog Haüs is proud to feature various amenities to provide both you and your beloved canine companion with the most pet-friendly accomodation to be experienced in Austin.

COMING MARCH 2017: The Dog Haüs plans to feature the first-ever canine exclusive water park! The perfect way to enjoy Austin's legendary summer weather.

The Dog Haüs offers the following amenities:

  • Locally roasted coffee, and organic dog treats
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Free parking for the duration of your stay
  • A 40 Acre dog park across the street
  • Hourly shuttles from 7:00am until 10:00pm between Town Lake and Dog Haüs
  • Daily room and laundry service
  • And of course, our highly-trained, friendly, and knowledgable staff

Additionally, every one of our suites features the following:

  • King-sized luxury mattresses
  • Memory foam dog beds and cushions
  • Complimentary mini-bar, it's on the Haüs
  • 48 inch flat-screen TV with 300+ channels
  • A safebox for your valuables
  • Plush locally-sourced furniture
  • Canine-friendly soaps and shampoos

Our Deluxe suites offer all of the above, as well as the following:

  • The best views the Dog Haüs has to offer
  • State-of-the-Art massage chairs for both yourself and your dog
  • A King-sized Jacuzzi
  • Hammocks on the patio
  • Additionally, all Deluxe Suite bookings come with a complimentary gift card to our Spa
